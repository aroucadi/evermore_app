# Evermore Jobs To Be Done Canvas

---

## Primary Job: Senior User

### Core Job Statement

> **When I** have stories and memories I want to preserve,  
> **I want to** share them with someone who listens and remembers,  
> **So I can** feel heard and know my life will be remembered.

---

### Functional Jobs

| Job | Importance | Current Solution | Satisfaction |
|-----|------------|------------------|--------------|
| Tell my life stories to someone who listens | Critical | Phone calls to family (rushed) | Low |
| Remember details from my past | High | Photo albums, notes | Medium |
| Share my history with grandchildren | Critical | Occasional visits, no system | Low |
| Have daily meaningful conversation | High | Senior center, TV | Low |
| Capture stories before I forget them | Critical | Nothing | None |

---

### Emotional Jobs

| Job | Context |
|-----|---------|
| Feel valued and heard | After days without meaningful conversation |
| Feel like my life mattered | When reflecting on accomplishments |
| Reduce anxiety about forgetting | When I notice memory lapses |
| Feel connected to family | When children are far away |
| Maintain dignity and purpose | When aging feels like decline |

---

### Social Jobs

| Job | Context |
|-----|---------|
| Be seen as a contributor, not a burden | When asking family for time |
| Leave a legacy for future generations | When thinking about mortality |
| Have something to share with visitors | When family or friends visit |

---

### Job Map (Senior)

| Stage | Job | Pains | Desired Outcome |
|-------|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Trigger** | Something reminds me of the past | No one around to share with | Have someone to tell immediately |
| **Preparation** | Decide to share a story | Forget by the time I can tell someone | Easy, immediate start |
| **Execution** | Tell the story | Listener rushed, interrupts, disengaged | Patient, curious listener |
| **Monitoring** | See if listener understood | No feedback, listener distracted | Follow-up questions, engagement |
| **Completion** | Story is shared | Ephemeral, forgotten | Story persisted, accessible to family |
| **Follow-up** | Continue relationship | Long gaps between conversations | Consistent presence, remembers context |

---

## Secondary Job: Family Member

### Core Job Statement

> **When I** am far from my aging parent,  
> **I want to** stay connected to their stories and wellbeing,  
> **So I can** preserve our family heritage and reduce my guilt.

---

### Functional Jobs

| Job | Importance | Current Solution | Satisfaction |
|-----|------------|------------------|--------------|
| Know my parent is engaged and stimulated | High | Phone calls, worry | Low |
| Capture parent's stories for posterity | Critical | Nothing systematic | None |
| Share parent's stories with my children | High | Occasional visits | Low |
| Monitor parent's cognitive/emotional state | Medium | Infer from calls | Low |
| Give parent a gift that provides ongoing value | Medium | Gadgets they don't use | Low |

---

### Emotional Jobs

| Job | Context |
|-----|---------|
| Reduce guilt about distance | When I can't visit as often as I want |
| Feel like I'm doing something for my parent | When writing checks isn't enough |
| Connect to my heritage | When I realize I don't know parent's history |
| Prepare for eventual loss | When mortality feels closer |

---

### Job Map (Family)

| Stage | Job | Pains | Desired Outcome |
|-------|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Awareness** | Realize parent's stories are fading | No wake-up call until crisis | Proactive prompt to act |
| **Evaluation** | Find a solution | Complex, expensive options | Simple, affordable tool |
| **Setup** | Get parent started | Tech friction, parent resistance | Effortless onboarding |
| **Ongoing** | Stay informed | No visibility into engagement | Dashboard with regular updates |
| **Retrieval** | Access stories | Stories locked in parent's memory | Searchable, shareable archive |
| **Legacy** | Pass stories to next generation | No system | Exportable, printable formats |

## Hiring Criteria

When would a user "hire" Evermore?

### Senior

| Trigger | Context |
|---------|---------|
| Lonely afternoon | No one to talk to, photos trigger memories |
| Family prompts | Grandchild sends birthday message with question |
| After a trigger event | Saw something that reminded them of childhood |

### Family

| Trigger | Context |
|---------|---------|
| Parent's birthday | Looking for meaningful gift |
| Health scare | Urgency to capture stories |
| Holidays | Realizing how little they know about parent's past |
| Another parent passes | Regret about not capturing stories |

---

## Competitive Jobs

Jobs that compete for attention:

| Competing Job | Competitor | When Evermore Wins |
|---------------|-----------|------------------|
| Be entertained | TV, radio | When user wants engagement, not passivity |
| Talk to family | Phone call | When family is unavailable |
| Write memoir | Journal, apps | When writing is too difficult |
| Nothing | Isolation | When Evermore is proactive |
