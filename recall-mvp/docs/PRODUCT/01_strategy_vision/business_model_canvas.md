# Recall Business Model Canvas

---

## Customer Segments

### Segment 1: Seniors (End Users)

- Age 65+, living independently or in assisted care
- Experiencing mild to moderate memory changes
- Want meaningful daily engagement
- May not control purchasing decisions

### Segment 2: Family Members (Buyers)

- Adult children of seniors, typically 35-60
- Motivated by legacy preservation and connection
- Geographic distance from elderly parent
- Willing to pay for senior wellbeing solutions

### Segment 3: Senior Care Facilities (B2B)

- Assisted living communities
- Memory care units
- Adult day programs
- Seeking engagement tools for residents

---

## Value Propositions

| Segment | Value Delivered |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Seniors** | A patient, empathetic companion that helps them remember, feel heard, and preserve their legacy |
| **Family** | Connection to parent's stories, peace of mind, and a tangible inheritance beyond possessions |
| **Facilities** | Scalable resident engagement that improves wellbeing metrics and family satisfaction |

## Revenue Streams

### Consumer Model

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | $0 | 3 sessions/week, 30-day memory |
| **Family Premium** | $19.99/month | Unlimited sessions, permanent memory, family dashboard, story exports |
| **Family Plus** | $29.99/month | Premium + 3 family contributor seats |

### Enterprise Model

| Model | Price |
|-------|-------|
| Per-resident licensing | $15/resident/month |
| Facility-wide unlimited | Custom annual contract |
